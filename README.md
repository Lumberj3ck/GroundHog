GROUNDHOG

A GROUNDHOG is AI assistant that helps you manage your day seamlessly.
It takes your notes or journal entries as input, uses Ollama GPT and other AI models to understand your context, and automatically creates and updates events in your Google Calendar.
The agent also tracks your daily progress, ensuring your tasks align with your goals.

Overview

The AI Day Planner Agent is designed to act as a personalized productivity companion.
With AI-powered natural language understanding and seamless calendar integration, it can:

Parse free-form text (notes, thoughts, journals) into structured plans.

Schedule meetings, reminders, and events in Google Calendar.

Track daily progress and provide productivity insights.

Adapt over time to your routines and preferences.

Features

Journal/Notes Input — Understands plain text entries and converts them into actionable tasks.

AI Model Integration — Works with Ollama GPT, OpenAI, and local LLMs.

Google Calendar Sync — Adds, updates, and monitors events automatically.

Daily Progress Tracker — Monitors how your day unfolds against your plan.

Extensible Interface — Add more models or data sources easily.
